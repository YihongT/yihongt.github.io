Research Opportunity Map

This map ranks possible paper ideas produced by combining forecasting agents, deep research, and agent safety/security references.

#1 — Predictive safety layer for deep forecasting agents

Best overall opportunity. Build a forecasting deep-research agent that predicts future unsafe or unreliable states in its own reasoning process. The model forecasts both the target time series and the risk trajectory of its context/tool/reasoning path.

  • Novelty: Moves from reactive guardrails to proactive risk forecasting.
  • Demand: Matches current interest in agents, safety, and reliable forecasting.
  • Feasibility: Can be evaluated with existing forecasting benchmarks plus adversarial context perturbations.

#2 — Safe context selection for forecasting

Use safety-aware retrieval and ranking to select evidence that is both predictive and robust. This is cleaner and easier than a full agent, but potentially less ambitious.

#3 — Forecasting-agent security benchmark

Create an adversarial benchmark for long-context forecasting agents. This is useful infrastructure, but it may need a method contribution to reach top-conference strength.

Recommended paper shape

Lead with SafeCAFDR as a method, include a benchmark slice for adversarial forecasting context, and report both forecasting accuracy and safety robustness. This creates a clearer contribution than a benchmark-only or safety-only paper.
